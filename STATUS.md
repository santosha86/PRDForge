## Honest scope

PRDForge is a **personal R&D project** I'm building in the open.

It exists because I've watched enough multi-agent projects collapse under their own weight to want a tool that says "you don't need this complexity" before the engineering starts.

The Suitability Gate concept is the part I'm most confident about — it's already validated across the multi-agent systems I've built (GhostCheck, Fleet Dispatch AI, AI Interview Platform, ContentOS). Each of those passed the bar. PRDForge is the codification of that bar.
